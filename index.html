Content

Word Clouds are visual displays done in graphics of text data presentations shown in various sizes and colors.
Each word has a different size that has a ratio with its frequency or significance in the text.
The larger sized words are the most common and frequently used.
Otherwise, words not used very often are shown in smaller font sizes.
Word Clouds help provide a clear and efficient visualization of the important keywords, topics, and main ideas within the text.
We use Word Clouds for text mining, data analysis, and other visual communication or summarizing of text data that is engaging (Amcharts).
